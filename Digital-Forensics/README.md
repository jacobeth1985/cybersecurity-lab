# Digital Forensics Lab

Welcome to my Digital Forensics Lab.

---

# About This Lab

Digital Forensics is the process of identifying, preserving, collecting, examining, analyzing, and presenting digital evidence. This lab demonstrates my practical experience using forensic methodologies, tools, and best practices to investigate cybersecurity incidents.
